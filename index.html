Frequently Asked Questions

Real answers, no jargon, no fluff.

How much does a resume cost?
Do I have to sign up for a subscription?

Hard pass. We don’t do commitment issues. You pay once, get your resume, and move on with your job search, and your life.

Is the resume really written by AI?

Yes, but not the kind that sounds like a sci-fi villain. Our AI writes like a human who knows what hiring managers want, minus the awkward phrasing and resume clichés.

Will my resume work with Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)?

You bet. Our resumes are formatted to charm the bots first, so you make it to the humans next.

Can I edit my resume after downloading it?

Of course. It’s your resume, you can tweak, tailor, or totally redo it in Word whenever you want.

How long does it take to build a resume?

Roughly the time it takes to microwave leftovers. Most users are done in under 10 minutes.

What if I don’t have much experience?

That’s our specialty. Whether you’re just starting out or pivoting careers, we know how to make what you do have shine like a pro.

Is my personal information secure?

Locked up tighter than your grandma’s cookie jar. We don’t store your info, and we definitely don’t sell it.

Can I create multiple resumes?

Yes! Want one for marketing and another for your secret dream of becoming a park ranger? Go for it. You only pay when you download.

What if I need help?

We’re actual people, friendly ones, even(most of the time). Email us anytime and we’ll make sure you’re taken care of.

Can I preview my resume before I pay?

Absolutely. We wouldn’t ask you to buy a car without test-driving it, and we feel the same about your resume. Build it, preview it, love it, then pay.

What makes Jobsprk different from other resume sites?

Glad you asked. No bloated pricing. No ugly templates. No “Oops, pay more to actually download.” Just sleek design, smart writing, and total honesty.

Can I use Jobsprk to tailor my resume to different positions?

Not yet, right now, Jobsprk creates a strong, general-purpose resume. But tailoring for specific roles? That’s on our roadmap, and we’re working on it. Stay tuned… it’s going to be good.

Do I get access to different templates or formats?

You do. We’ve curated designs that walk the fine line between stylish and ATS-safe. Basically: templates that look good and play by the rules.

Will my resume sound like everyone else's?

No chance. Our AI adapts to your input, your tone, and your experience. It’s more “custom-built” than “cut and paste.”

Can I use Jobsprk on my phone or tablet?

Not just yet. We’re desktop-first for now, but mobile optimization is in the works. Soon, you’ll be able to build your resume from your phone and ignore texts from your ex, all at once.

What file formats do I get?

You’ll get a clean, editable .docx (Microsoft Word) file, no PDFs locked in stone, no weird formatting drama.

Can I update my resume later without starting from scratch?

Yes. Once it’s downloaded, it’s yours forever. Edit it anytime, however you like. No need to rebuild the wheel.

Do you offer cover letters too?

Not yet, but we’re cooking on it. For now, we’re laser-focused on making your resume the MVP of your job hunt.

Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied?

Since it's a digital download, all sales are final. But if something's off, reach out, we're humans, and we'll make it right.
